## Ingredients

**Cheesecake Filling:**
- 24 oz (680g) cream cheese, room temperature
- 1 can (15 oz/425g) Libby's Pumpkin Pie Mix
- ¾ cup (180ml) evaporated milk
- 3 large eggs
- ¼ cup (50g) sugar

**Graham Cracker Topping:**
- 1 cup (100g) graham cracker crumbs
- 3 tbsp (45g) butter, melted
- 2 tbsp (25g) sugar

## Instructions

1. **Preheat:** Set Anova sous vide to 176°F.

2. **Make filling:** Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth with no lumps. Add pumpkin pie mix and beat until combined. Add evaporated milk and mix until smooth. Add eggs one at a time on low speed to minimize air bubbles. Blend until completely uniform.

3. **Fill jars:** Pour filling into 8oz mason jars, leaving ¾-1" headspace. Wipe rims clean. Screw lids on finger-tight (not fully tight—allows pressure release).

4. **Sous vide:** Submerge jars using weighted rack to prevent floating. Cook at 176°F for 90 minutes. Filling will look loose and jiggly when done—this is correct.

5. **Cool:** Remove jars and cool 20-30 minutes at room temperature, then refrigerate for minimum 4 hours (overnight is ideal).

6. **Serve:** Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Sprinkle on jars right before serving for maximum crunch.

## Notes

- Room temperature cream cheese is essential for smooth filling without lumps
- Don't overtighten jar lids—pressure needs to escape during cooking
- Jiggly filling when done is normal—it firms up during refrigeration
- Make graham topping fresh before serving to keep it crunchy
- Can be made 2-3 days ahead (add topping just before serving)
- Perfect for individual portions at holiday gatherings

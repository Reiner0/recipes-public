## Ingredients

**Cake:**
- 1 box Betty Crocker Super Moist White Cake Mix
- 5 limes (zested and juiced — zest all before juicing)
- Bottled lime juice (to supplement if needed)
- Buttermilk (in place of remaining liquid called for on box)
- Eggs and oil as directed on box

**Frosting:**
- 1 container store-bought whipped cream cheese frosting
- Lime zest to taste (stirred in + reserved for garnish)

**Glaze (alternative):**
- 1 cup (120g) powdered sugar
- 2–3 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp lime zest

## Instructions

1. **Prep limes:** Zest all 5 limes before juicing — this is much easier. Set zest aside.

2. **Mix batter:** Prepare box mix per package directions with these swaps:
   - Replace ~half the water with lime juice (fresh or bottled)
   - Replace remaining water with buttermilk
   - Fold all lime zest into the batter
   - Use eggs and oil as directed on box

3. **Prep bundt pan:** Grease every crevice thoroughly with butter or shortening rubbed in with a paper towel, then dust lightly with flour. Spray alone isn't enough for bundt pans.

4. **Bake:** Pour batter in and bake at temperature and time directed on box. Check for doneness with a toothpick — it should come out clean.

5. **Cool and unmold:** Let cool in pan 10–15 minutes, then invert onto a rack. Cool completely at room temperature (1–2 hours) before glazing or frosting.

6. **Make-ahead option:** Wrap cooled cake and refrigerate overnight. Cold cake is easier to handle and lime flavor deepens as it sits. Pull out 20–30 minutes before serving.

7. **Frost:** Stir lime zest directly into the cream cheese frosting — this gets lime flavor into every bite rather than just on top. Spread or drizzle over the cooled cake, then garnish with extra zest.

   **Glaze alternative:** Whisk powdered sugar with lime juice until pourable. Drizzle over the cake while still slightly warm so it soaks into the surface. Finish with a scatter of zest.

## Notes

- **Zest is key:** Most of the bold lime flavor comes from zest, not juice. Don't reduce it.
- **Bundt tip:** Thorough greasing is critical — butter/shortening + flour dusting beats spray alone every time.
- **Cream cheese + lime:** Mix zest into the frosting rather than just sprinkling — you get lime in every bite.
- **Storage:** Refrigerate covered up to 3 days. The flavor actually improves overnight.

## Ingredients

**Cookies:**
- 1 cup (225g) butter, softened
- ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar
- 1 tsp (5ml) vanilla extract
- 2 cups (240g) all-purpose flour

**Optional Sprinkles:**
- 2 tbsp (30ml) milk
- ¼ cup sprinkles

## Instructions

1. **Make dough:** Beat butter and sugar in large bowl with electric mixer until fluffy. Stir in vanilla and flour and mix until well combined.

2. **Cut shapes:** Roll dough to ½ inch thick and use cookie cutter to create shapes (3-inch round cutter makes about 24 cookies).

3. **Chill:** Place cookies on parchment paper-lined baking sheets and chill in fridge for 30 minutes.

4. **Preheat oven** to 350°F (175°C).

5. **Bake:** Bake 12-18 minutes. Set timer for 10 minutes and check every 2 minutes after that. Cookies are done when they just begin to get golden brown on the sides.

6. **Add sprinkles (optional):** Brush just enough milk on each cookie to get top slightly wet, then scatter sprinkles over it. The milk helps them stick. Let cool slightly to prevent sprinkles from bleeding onto cookie.

7. **Serve:** Serve with cookie dip or by themselves.

## Notes

- Chilling after cutting (vs. rolling chilled dough) makes handling much easier
- Don't overbake—shortbread should be pale with just slight browning on edges
- Cookies will firm up as they cool
- Can freeze unbaked dough for up to 3 months; thaw before rolling and cutting
- Classic shortbread ratio: 1 part sugar, 2 parts butter, 3 parts flour

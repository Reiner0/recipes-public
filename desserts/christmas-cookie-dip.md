## Ingredients

- ½ cup (120g) Greek vanilla yogurt
- 4 oz (115g) cream cheese, softened
- 3 tbsp (45g) butter, softened
- 1 tsp (5ml) vanilla extract
- ¼-½ cup (30-60g) powdered sugar
- Red and green sprinkles

**For Dipping:**
- Shortbread cookies
- Fresh fruit (strawberries, apple slices)
- Pretzels

## Instructions

1. **Beat base:** In large bowl, use electric mixer to beat yogurt, cream cheese, and butter until light and fluffy, about 5 minutes.

2. **Add sweetener:** Add vanilla and ¼ cup powdered sugar. Beat until well combined.

3. **Taste and adjust:** Take a taste. If you want sweeter dip, add ⅛-¼ cup more powdered sugar and beat again.

4. **Decorate:** Top with red and green sprinkles.

5. **Serve:** Serve with cookies, fruit, and/or pretzels.

## Notes

- Room temperature cream cheese and butter are essential for smooth, fluffy texture
- Start with less sugar—easier to add more than to fix over-sweetened dip
- Can be made 1-2 days ahead; store covered in refrigerator
- Pairs perfectly with shortbread cookies (see recipe in this collection)
- Greek yogurt adds tanginess that balances sweetness

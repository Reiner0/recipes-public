## Ingredients

- 1 pound 85% lean ground beef
- 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables (such as corn, okra, carrots, peas, and green beans)
- 8 ounces tomato sauce
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 tablespoon chicken broth base
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons black pepper
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Instructions

1. **Brown beef:** Select SAUTE on the Instant Pot. When the pot is hot, add the beef and cook, stirring just enough to break up the clumps, for 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in the frozen vegetables, tomato sauce, vinegar, tomato paste, chicken broth base, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Select CANCEL.

2. **Pressure cook:** Secure the lid on the pot. Close the pressure-release valve. Select MANUAL and set the pot at HIGH pressure for 5 minutes. At the end of the cooking time, allow the pot to sit undisturbed for 10 minutes, then release any remaining pressure.

3. **Finish and serve:** Stir in the lemon juice and serve.

## Notes

What to make when you have ground beef, a freezer full of vegetables, no plan, and hungry people. This very versatile recipe lets you put together a very tasty stew with the simplest ingredients.

Sometimes you need to go shopping for special ingredients for a dish, but other times you just need to use what you have.

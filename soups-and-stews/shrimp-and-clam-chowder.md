## Ingredients

### For the Soup
- 6 slices bacon, chopped
- 4 cups cubed potatoes (½-inch dice)
- 1½ cups chopped yellow onions
- 1 (8-ounce) bottle clam juice, or 1 cup chicken or fish broth
- 1 cup water
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 3 sprigs fresh thyme, or 1 teaspoon dried
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 bay leaves

### For Finishing
- ½ cup water (optional)
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch (optional)
- 1 (14-ounce) can clams, undrained
- 1 cup cooked salad shrimp
- 1 cup heavy cream
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. **Combine soup ingredients:** In the Instant Pot, combine the bacon, potatoes, onions, clam juice, water, garlic, thyme, salt, pepper, and bay leaves. Stir to mix well.

2. **Pressure cook:** Secure the lid on the pot. Close the pressure-release valve. Select MANUAL and set the pot at HIGH pressure for 6 minutes. At the end of the cooking time, quick release the pressure. Discard the bay leaves. Use a potato masher to coarsely mash and thicken the soup.

3. **Finish soup:** Select SAUTE. If using cornstarch, in a small bowl, stir together the water and cornstarch to make a slurry; add the slurry to the soup and bring to a boil. Add the clams and their juices, the shrimp, cream, and parsley. Cook, stirring occasionally, until heated through and thickened slightly, 1 to 2 minutes. Select CANCEL. Serve.

## Notes

This recipe is inspired by slumgullion, a cream chowder perked up with small cooked shrimp. That means many things to many people, but here it means delicious seafood chowder.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ancho chile powder
- ½ teaspoon black pepper
- 3 pounds boneless pork shoulder, cut into 5 large pieces
- 1 cup water
- Corn tortillas, for serving

### Optional Toppings
- Salsa verde
- Sour cream
- Diced avocado
- Fresh cilantro
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

1. **Season pork:** In a large bowl, combine the brown sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, ancho chile powder, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Add the pork to the bowl and toss to coat, massaging the spices into the meat. Allow to stand at room temperature for 30 minutes.

2. **Add to pot:** Transfer the meat to the Instant Pot. Add the water.

3. **Pressure cook:** Secure the lid on the pot. Close the pressure-release valve. Select MANUAL and set the pot at HIGH pressure for 25 minutes. At the end of the cooking time, allow the pot to sit undisturbed for 10 minutes, then release any remaining pressure.

4. **Shred pork:** Using two forks, shred the meat, making sure it's submerged under the cooking liquid until ready to serve.

5. **Serve:** Serve the shredded pork in corn tortillas with any desired toppings.

## Notes

This homemade rub makes absolutely delicious Mexican pork tacos. While the recipe works well in a smoker, this Instant Pot version is perfect for last-minute meals when you need delicious taco filling for burritos, tacos, or other creative uses.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons salted butter
- 4 garlic cloves, thickly sliced
- 1 cup sliced yellow onions
- 2 cups quartered button mushrooms
- 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 3 sprigs fresh thyme, or 1 teaspoon dried
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 cups baby spinach
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

## Instructions

1. **Sauté aromatics:** Select SAUTE on the Instant Pot. When the pot is hot, add the butter. When the butter has melted, add the garlic. Cook, stirring continuously, until garlic is fragrant and slightly softened, about 1 minute (take care not to let it burn).

2. **Add vegetables and chicken:** Add the onions and mushrooms and stir to coat. Add the chicken, thyme, water, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine. Place the spinach on top of the chicken mixture. Select CANCEL.

3. **Pressure cook:** Secure the lid on the pot. Close the pressure-release valve. Select MANUAL and set the pot at HIGH pressure for 8 minutes. At the end of the cooking time, allow the pot to sit undisturbed for 10 minutes, then release any remaining pressure.

4. **Keep chicken warm:** Transfer the chicken to a serving platter. Cover lightly with aluminum foil to keep warm.

5. **Make cream sauce:** Select SAUTE on the Instant Pot. While stirring continuously, slowly add the cream in a steady stream. Cook, stirring, until the mixture starts to thicken, 5 to 8 minutes. Stir in the lemon juice. Select CANCEL.

6. **Serve:** Serve the chicken with the mushroom-spinach sauce.

## Notes

No need for canned cream of mushroom soup here. This homemade version is healthier and just as comforting. The spinach cooks down and blends in with everything else, giving you a complete vegetable and chicken dish in one pot.

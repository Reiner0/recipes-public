## Ingredients

**Cheese Filling:**
- 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
- 1 large egg
- 2 cups (225g) pre-shredded mozzarella
- ½ cup (50g) fresh grated parmesan
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp pepper

**Meat Sauce:**
- 1 lb Italian sausage
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 32 oz Rao's marinara sauce

**Lasagna:**
- 12 lasagna noodles
- 1½ cups (170g) pre-shredded mozzarella

## Instructions

1. **Cook pasta:** Bring large pot of salted water to boil. Cook lasagna noodles to al dente per package directions. Drain, rinse with cold water. Line up on parchment or wax paper.

2. **Cook sausage:** In large skillet over medium-high heat, cook Italian sausage, breaking it up finely with spatula, 8-10 minutes until cooked through. Add garlic during last minute. Drain excess grease.

3. **Prep baking dish:** Spread ½ cup marinara on bottom of 9x13" baking dish. Preheat oven to 375°F.

4. **Make sauce:** Add remaining marinara sauce to sausage. Bring to boil, then reduce to simmer.

5. **Make cheese filling:** Combine ricotta, egg, 2 cups mozzarella, parmesan, oregano, salt, and pepper in bowl.

6. **Assemble:** Spread 2½ tbsp cheese mixture evenly over each noodle. Top with ¼ cup meat sauce. Roll up tightly, keeping filling inside. Place seam-side down in prepared dish.

7. **Top:** Cover rolls with remaining meat sauce. Sprinkle with 1½ cups mozzarella.

8. **Bake:** Spray foil with cooking spray, place spray-side down over dish. Bake 35 minutes. Remove foil, broil at 450°F for 3-5 minutes to brown top. Watch carefully.

9. **Rest:** Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

## Notes

- Broiling the top at end gives better cheese texture
- Can assemble ahead and refrigerate up to 2 days; add 10 min to bake time if cold
- Can freeze for up to 3 months

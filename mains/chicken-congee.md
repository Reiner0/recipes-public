## Ingredients

- 6 cups water
- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
- 1 cup jasmine rice, rinsed and drained
- 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil, plus more for garnish
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper (optional)
- Chopped scallions, for garnish

## Instructions

1. **Combine ingredients:** In the Instant Pot, combine the water, chicken, rice, ginger, garlic, sesame oil, soy sauce, salt, and pepper (if using).

2. **Pressure cook:** Secure the lid on the pot. Close the pressure-release valve. Select the PORRIDGE setting (see Note). At the end of the cooking time, allow the pot to sit undisturbed for 10 minutes, then release any remaining pressure.

3. **Serve:** Stir to combine the ingredients. Divide among four bowls. Drizzle each with toasted sesame oil, sprinkle with scallions, and serve.

## Notes

If you don't have a Porridge function on your Instant Pot, select MANUAL and set at HIGH pressure for 20 minutes.

Add water after it's cooked for a thinner congee, if desired. This is perfect comfort food for an upset stomach or any day you need something warm and soothing.

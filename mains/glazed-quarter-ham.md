## Ingredients

**Ham:**
- 2 quarter hams

**Glaze:**
- 3 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp dijon mustard
- Pinch black pepper

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 325°F.

2. **Cook ham** uncovered for 30 minutes until internal temperature reaches 140°F.

3. **Make glaze:** Melt brown sugar, butter, honey, dijon mustard, and black pepper together in small saucepan.

4. **Glaze and finish:** Brush glaze on ham. Increase oven to 400°F and broil for 15 minutes until glaze is caramelized and ham is golden.

## Notes

- Watch carefully during broiling to prevent burning
- Internal temp should reach 140°F (ham is pre-cooked, you're just heating and glazing)

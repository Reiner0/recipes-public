## Ingredients

- 1 lb (450g) broccoli florets
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp pepper
- 3 cloves garlic, minced

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 425°F.

2. **Prep broccoli:** Wash broccoli and pat completely dry.

3. **Season:** Place broccoli on baking sheet (preferably dark) and drizzle with olive oil. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and garlic. Toss to coat.

4. **Arrange:** Spread in even layer on sheet without overlapping so edges get crisp.

5. **First bake:** Bake for 10 minutes and remove from oven. Flip florets over and redistribute around baking sheet.

6. **Finish:** Bake for 5-10 more minutes until desired color is achieved (18 minutes total gives good browning). Remove and serve with lemon wedges if desired.

## Notes

- Dark baking sheet promotes better browning
- Drying broccoli thoroughly is key to getting crispy edges instead of steaming
- Can adjust temp to 450°F; total bake time reduces to ~15 minutes
- Watch closely at higher temp to prevent burning

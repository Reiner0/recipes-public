## Ingredients

- 1 lb (450g) elbow macaroni pasta
- 4 cups (950ml) chicken broth
- 2 tsp dry mustard
- ½ tsp seasoned salt
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp pepper
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 cups (340g) shredded cheese (mix of cheddar & mozzarella works well)
- 1 cup (240ml) whole milk

## Instructions

1. **Prep dairy:** Set shredded cheese, butter, and milk aside at room temperature. They shouldn't be cold when added to the macaroni.

2. **Pressure cook:** Add chicken broth and seasonings to Instant Pot and mix until well combined. Pour in macaroni. Secure lid and ensure quick release valve is closed. Pressure cook for 5 minutes.

3. **Quick release:** Flip quick release valve when finished to allow steam to escape and stop cooking process immediately.

4. **Add cheese:** Stir in butter, cheese, and milk until well combined and creamy.

5. **Serve:** Sprinkle with parsley and serve.

## Notes

- Bringing dairy to room temp prevents clumping and ensures smooth, creamy sauce
- Store in refrigerator for 3-5 days
- Can freeze in airtight container for up to 3 months

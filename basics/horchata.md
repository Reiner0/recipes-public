## Ingredients

- 32 ounces (4 cups) unsweetened rice milk
- 6 tablespoons sugar or sweetener of choice
- 1 cinnamon stick, broken
- Ice cubes, for serving

## Instructions

1. **Combine ingredients:** In the Instant Pot, combine the rice milk, sugar, and cinnamon.

2. **Pressure cook:** Secure the lid on the pot. Close the pressure-release valve. Select MANUAL and set the pot at HIGH pressure for 4 minutes. At the end of the cooking time, allow the pot to sit undisturbed for 10 minutes, then release any remaining pressure.

3. **Chill and serve:** Refrigerate until chilled or serve in glasses over ice.

## Notes

This recipe uses ready-made rice milk as a shortcut for authentic horchata taste with minimal effort. Traditional methods involve soaking and grinding rice, but this approach delivers the same delicious result in a fraction of the time.

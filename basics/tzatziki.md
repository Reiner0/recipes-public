## Ingredients

- 1 large cucumber, peeled and grated (about 2 cups)
- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tablespoon tahini (sesame paste)
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste
- Chopped fresh parsley or dill, for garnish

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together the cucumber, yogurt, garlic, tahini, lemon juice, and salt until well combined. Cover and chill until ready to serve.

2. **Right:** before serving, sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley or dill, if desired.

## Notes

NOTE: It's not traditional to use tahini in tzatziki, but it creates a much smoother dip.
